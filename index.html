The thesis

Ebitdaa Ventures builds software at the intersection of consumer behavior and operational intelligence. Every product starts with a real problem — felt firsthand — and ships with the speed and precision of a team that knows what good looks like from the inside.

The self-storage operating system wasn't designed by a product team in a conference room. It was built by operators managing 20+ facilities who got tired of the tools that were supposed to help.

Built by Operators
For the Modern Entrepreneur

Ebitdaa Ventures was founded by entrepreneurs who believe the best software comes from people who live inside the problem. We build tools for operators, decision-makers, and everyday consumers — the people legacy software forgot. Every product we ship is designed to move fast, stay lean, and solve real friction.
